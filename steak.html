Beef Steak

Prep Time: 35 minutes

Yield: 4


Cook the steak on one side for 3-6 minutes. The length of time you should cook your steak depends on your preferred temperature and particular cut of steak. On average, each side should brown for about 5 minutes. If you prefer a more pink steak, cook each side for less time.
